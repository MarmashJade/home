Travels

I'm very lucky, I have been able to travel to some very unique and spectacular places in my life. These are some my favourite places I've visited:

Hawaii

In the summer of 2018, my family and I went to Big Island, Hawaiifor a week. It was a very memorable trip for a few reasons. The first reason being that it was super beautiful there. We visited several volcanic rock beaches and I went snorkelling for the first time. The weather was also really nice and I was able to try a lot of new foods. The other reason was that Big Island was hit by a really big hurricane, the first in decades. Luckily, on the side of the island that we were staying on, we weren't too heavily impacted but a lot of the things we wanted to do were closed in preparation for the hurricane. Even though we had to cancel a lot of our plans, it was still an amazing experience and I would really like to go back some day.

Rockies

During March break of 2018 my family and I went on a ski trip with my uncle in the Rockies. We mainly skied atPanorama ski resortand it was one of the most beautiful places I have ever skied. The views from the top of the mountain were absolutely stunning and so many different ski trails to try out. I did my first double diamond trail there and it was awesome because there was fresh, untouched powder on that slope. Additionally, we also visited Banff National Park. The beauty of the Rockies is truly indescribable and the grandness of them really doesn't translate in pictures. The Rockies are truly amazing geographical wonders.

Taiwan

My mom is from Taiwan so we have gone back there and visited her family several times. Recently we haven't been visiting as much though. The most recent trip we took was back in 2014. I have a lot of really great memories of my visits to Taiwan from visiting arcades with my uncle, to shopping at the night markets with my aunts to visiting Taipei zoo and taking the high speed trains and MRT. I really hope to go back there some time soon because I like feeling connected to my mom's family and culture by visiting the places she grew up in.

South Africa

My visit to South Africa was truly one of the most special trips I've ever been on. In the summer of 2015 we got on a 20-hour flight with my grandparents and then drove 7 hours from the Johannesburg airport to Kruger National parkwhere we met up with my uncle and aunt. Over the course of the 2 weeks that we stayed there we saw all kinds of amazing wild animals from lions and leopards to rhinos and elephants. We also visited an elephant sanctuary where we got to ride an elephant! This was a really special trip, not only because I got so many new experiences and saw so many cool things, but also because I met my aunt for the first time on that trip.
